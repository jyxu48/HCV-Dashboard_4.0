How to Use

  • Use the AMI Threshold toggles to filter by income bracket (<30%, 30–50%, or 50–80% of AMI).
  • Switch between Raw Gap and weighted variants — weighted options include renters, children, elderly, households of color, and poverty.
  • Click any state to drill into county-level data; select a county to load census tract detail.
  • The sidebar updates with gap statistics, eligible renter counts, and voucher coverage for the selected geography.
