The Voucher Gap Ratio, Weighted by Renter Population,
in the 25 Largest U.S. Metros
Among the 25 most populous metropolitan areas. Weighted Gap = Voucher Gap Ratio × Renter Share, where Gap Ratio = (Eligible Renters − Vouchers) ÷ Total Renters and Renter Share = Renters ÷ Total Households.
AMI THRESHOLD
Formula: Weighted Gap = Voucher Gap Ratio × Renter Share — where Gap Ratio = (Eligible Renters − Vouchers Under Lease) ÷ Total Renters, and Renter Share = Total Renters ÷ Total Households. Counties aggregated to MSA using Census CBSA delineations. Sources: HUD CHAS 2018–2022 5-year estimates (eligible renters); HUD Picture of Subsidized Households 2022 (vouchers); Census CBSA population estimates 2022.
